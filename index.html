Controls & Features

  • Always hold hand upright when drawing and rotating ☝️
  • Draw mode: Index finger up, middle finger down. Stop drawing by putting up your middle finger next to your index finger.
  • Straight Line Snap: Stop moving at the end of a stroke for a second.
  • Rotate Canvas: Make a fist and hold for 1.2 seconds (cursor turns yellow, then white). Drag hand to spin.
  • Shortcuts: Press C to Clear, E to Export, L to toggle 2D Lock.
